About

Hello, my name is Jeffrey Farnan.

I have been working in the CyberSecurity industry for the past 8+ years, starting as an Intern, Security Analyst and then advancing to Threat Research Analyst.

My daily routine involves making determinations in cloud threat research systems to analysing single files with inhouse and open source Malware Analysis tools.

I download suspected Malware to Virtual Machines doing a deep Malware Analysis and using Reverse Engineering methods to take the Malware apart to figure how it works. I write about what I have learned in various Threat Blogs.

I have a degree in Cybersecutity where I learned all the different aspects of the industry while gaining valuable skills needed to meet its ongoing challenges.

This blog covers these many different aspects of Cybersecurity while exploring the many different types of Malware available. Advanced skills are needed to understand how Malware works so I have provided turorials for beginner to the more advanced researcher wishing to research the latest Malware out today.
