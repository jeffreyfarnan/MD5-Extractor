Presentations


These presentations were created to inform security teams about the latest malware and ongoing threats that are currently active.

A.I Powered Cyber Attacks

We will explore what A.I is and its development, see how A.I and machine learning used by attackers to increase the speed, scale and sophistication of Cyber Attacks.



Rootkits

In this presentation we will explore at early development of rootkits and the most common popular types and how they work. Explore modern rootkits from virtual machine to firmware rootkits and looking at various samples.

CCleaner Attack
